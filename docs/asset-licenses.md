## Audio (`assets/audio/`)

**Placeholder, royalty-free tones — not final sound design.** The 10 WAVs
(`correct`, `fever`, `invalid`, `note_1`…`note_5`, `target`, `ui_tap`) are
procedurally synthesized 16-bit PCM tones generated by
`tool/gen_placeholder_audio.dart`. They are **CC0 / public domain** (own work, no
third-party samples, deterministic and reproducible byte-for-byte).

A licensed/designed sound set replaces these before store release; when it does,
record its source and license here.

## Branding (`assets/branding/`)

`icon.png`, `icon_foreground.png`, `splash.png` — the HEX • CALC app mark (neon hex +
equals on the near-black brand background). Own work (CC0), generated as the source for
`flutter_launcher_icons` / `flutter_native_splash`. Not bundled at runtime.
